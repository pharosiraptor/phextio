Help

You can explore other phexts by providing the multiversal seed. You can also quick jump by setting coordinates manually. Phext addresses are of this form:

<LB>.<SF>.<SR>/<CN>.<VM>.<BK>/<CH>.<SN>.<SC>

Where:

  • LB = Library
  • SF = Shelf
  • SR = Series
  • CN = Collection
  • VM = Volume
  • BK = Book
  • CH = Chapter
  • SN = Section
  • SC = Scroll
